The Dark Forest Character Guide

Concise profiles from Liu Cixin's masterpiece

Luo Ji

Astronomer turned Wallfacer who develops cosmic sociology theory. Initially reluctant hero evolves into humanity's strategic thinker[1][3][10]

Shi Qiang

Practical police officer turned security chief, provides grounded perspective amid cosmic threats. Luo Ji's loyal protector[1][4][10]

Zhang Beihai

Space Force officer who secretly prepares interstellar escape. Master strategist hiding defeatism behind triumphalist facade[1][10]

Ye Wenjie

ETO founder who shares cosmic sociology principles. Complex figure bridging human/Trisolaris communication[1][10]

Zhuang Yan

Artist and Luo Ji's idealized companion. Becomes emotional leverage in Wallfacer Project[1][2][10]
